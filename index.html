I am Vrushtee.

Mumbai, India

My life recently has been a lot of robotics. I love to learn and to make robots learn.

3D printed pieces put together with servos, cameras, and a lot of experimental models that rarely work.

Striving to optimize life. Striving to create, to bring ideas to real life.

Creating is about making asymmetric pieces spring into action—things that do not resonate, do not vibrate with each other. They just coalesce to form an object, a machine, or a device of great help.
